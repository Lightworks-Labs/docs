---
title: Databases
date: April 7, 2026
---

Databases are folders in your repository with a schema and a collection of records. Define properties with 12 typed fields — text, number, select, status, date, person, relation, esign, and more — then view records in Table, List, Board, Gallery, or Feed view.

Switch views from the toolbar, sort and filter in Table view, and drag cards between columns in Board view to update properties directly in the frontmatter.
